# Season Prize Pool

The Season Prize Pool was an early-beta feature that paid a self-funded SOL reward to the top 3 PvP players at the end of each 90-day season.

**This feature has been superseded by the BITS reward system.** The prize pool card no longer appears in the REWARDS app.

## What Replaced It

The REWARDS screen now shows:

* **BITS balance** — your accumulated BITS across all Snake OS apps
* **Claimable PvP SOL** — winnings from PvP matches and tournaments
* **BITS Exchange** — coming at token launch; stack BITS now to claim your proportional share of the weekly token burn pool

## PvP SOL Rewards Still Work

PvP and Tournament winnings are still paid in SOL and are claimable at any time from the REWARDS app. See [PvP Claims](pvp-claims.md).

## BITS Leaderboard Rewards

Top players on the leaderboard earn bonus BITS each week. The distribution is shown in the LEADERBOARD app. See [Leaderboard](leaderboard.md).

# Achievements & Badges

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-05-26 142627.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The BADGES app shows your achievement progress. Every achievement you unlock earns **BITS** — the Snake OS reward currency — when you claim it.

## How It Works

1. Progress toward each achievement automatically as you play and explore Snake OS
2. When an achievement's condition is met, it's marked **UNLOCKED**
3. Tap **CLAIM** to receive the BITS reward
4. BITS accumulate in your balance and convert to tokens at launch

No participation gate. No minimum spend. Just unlock and claim.

## What Earns BITS

Achievements span every part of Snake OS — not just the game:

| Category | Examples |
| -------- | -------- |
| **Gameplay** | Play PvP matches, win challenges, enter tournaments |
| **Capital** | Wager SOL, spend in the market, own cosmetics |
| **Skill** | Reach a high score, climb the leaderboard |
| **Social** | Add friends, send chat messages |
| **Explorer** | Use ORACLE, visit RADAR, trade through the wallet |

Top-tier achievements (rank #1, 100+ PvP wins, 10 SOL wagered) pay the most BITS — they're the hardest to earn.

## BITS → Token

BITS are a pre-launch accumulation currency. When the token launches, BITS convert proportionally from a weekly burn pool. The more BITS you hold at launch, the larger your share each week.

Stack now. Exchange opens at launch.

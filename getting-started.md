# Getting Started

## What is Snake OS?

Snake OS is a Solana-native app shaped like a Nokia 3310 — one retro phone shell with 25 apps: real-SOL Snake, pump.fun migration feed, AI token scanner, perp radar, tokenized stocks, global chat, and more.

**Three things you can do:**
- **Play** — Solo Snake to practice, or wager real SOL 1v1 against other players. Skill determines the winner — no RNG.
- **Discover** — DEGEN, SNIPE, ORACLE, and RADAR let you track Solana token launches, KOL moves, and perp positioning without leaving the phone.
- **Earn BITS** — every action earns BITS: gaming, trading, chatting, scanning, exploring. BITS carry forward to the token launch.

---

## Setup in 5 Minutes

**Step 1 — Connect your wallet**

Open [snake-os.com](https://snake-os.com) and tap CONNECT.

| Platform | Wallet |
|----------|--------|
| Desktop (Chrome/Brave/Firefox) | Phantom extension |
| iOS Safari | Phantom universal link |
| Android Chrome | Tap CONNECT → redirected into Phantom's in-app browser |
| Seeker (Solana Mobile) | MWA inside the dApp Store app |

You'll sign one message — *Snake OS — sign in* — no SOL spent, cached for 1 hour.

**Step 2 — Accept Terms**

TOS modal appears on first connect. Read it, then tap ACCEPT.

> The first 20 wallets to accept get a permanent **BETA badge** and every market item free for pre-launch. Slots are claimed atomically on TOS-accept.

**Step 3 — Set a username**

SETTINGS (home screen) → tap your address → pick a username (3–16 chars, alphanumeric + underscore).

**Step 4 — Play a game**

Press key `1` or tap the SNAKE icon. Use the d-pad to move, eat food to grow. Wall or tail = game over. Your high score saves to the leaderboard automatically.

**Step 5 — Explore and earn BITS**

Number keys 1–9 jump to apps. BACK (left softkey) returns home. Every game you play, scan you run, message you send, or trade you make earns BITS.

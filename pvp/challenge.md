# Challenge Mode

Challenge mode is a **solo, house-funded** betting game. Pick a tier, wager SOL, and beat a target score in a single life to win a multiplied payout — no opponent needed.

## How It Works

1. Open the **DEGEN** screen and tap the **CHALLENGE** tab
2. Pick your tier and wager amount
3. Send the wager to the Snake OS treasury via your wallet
4. The server reserves your potential profit from the challenge treasury **before** your game starts — your payout is guaranteed if you win
5. Play — **1 life, no powerups**
6. When you die, the server reads your score from the session and compares it to the target
   * **Win** → SOL paid directly to your wallet (no claim step needed)
   * **Loss** → your wager stays in the treasury

## Tiers

| Tier | Target Score | Payout | Food needed (~) |
|------|-------------|--------|-----------------|
| **EASY** | 6,000 pts | 1.5× your wager | ~80 food |
| **DEGEN** | 10,000 pts | 2× your wager | ~133 food |
| **INSANE** | 14,000 pts | 2.5× your wager | ~186 food |

**Wager range:** 0.05 SOL minimum — 0.20 SOL maximum per game.

## Scoring Reminder

Each food pellet = **75 pts**. Each game tick survived = **+1 pt**. EASY at 6,000 pts requires a solid, clean run at speed. INSANE at 14,000 is near-perfect territory.

## Key Rules

* **1 life** — one death ends the game regardless of score
* **No powerups** — pure skill, nothing to offset a mistake
* The challenge treasury must have sufficient balance to cover your potential win — if a tier shows as unavailable, the treasury is low for that multiplier. Try a smaller wager or a lower tier.
* Anti-cheat preflight runs at settle time. Flagged sessions score a loss.

## Treasury Funding

The challenge treasury is seeded manually and topped up by **50% of every PvP settle fee**. The more PvP volume, the deeper the challenge treasury and the larger wagers it can support.

## Win Payout

Challenge wins are paid **directly to your wallet** via on-chain transfer — no REWARDS claim step required. You'll see the TX signature on the result screen.

## Loss

Your wager stays in the treasury. The reserved profit is also returned to the treasury pool. No claim, no credit — the game resets.

The home screen is a 3-column grid of all apps. Tap any icon to open. The 5 most-used apps also have number-key shortcuts (see [Number Key Shortcuts](number-keys.md)).

Recent layout pass enlarged the app icons and labels in the 3-column grid — the previous tight pass was too small to read at arm's length on the phone shell. App icons now fill more of each cell with bigger labels underneath.

## Notification Badges

App icons show a red notification badge when they have new activity:

- **PVP** — pending match invites
- **CHAT** — unread messages
- **FRIENDS** — incoming requests
- **REWARDS** — claimable balance available
- **RADAR** — new whale events or OI surges (clears when you open the app)

Badges are sized at 20px disc with bold 12px text — recent bump from a smaller 14px / 9px disc that was hard to read at distance.

## Navigation Habits

* **D-pad** to move highlight between icons, **OK** to launch
* **Number keys** for shortcuts to the 5 marked apps
* **BACK** softkey or key **5** to return home from any app

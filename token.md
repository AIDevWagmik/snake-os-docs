## The Launch Mechanism

The token launches via pump.fun's fair-launch mechanism:

- **Total supply:** 1,000,000,000 (1 billion)
- **800M** on the bonding curve — anyone can buy at deployment
- **200M** auto-paired to LP when the curve completes (~$69k market cap)
- **No presale, no team allocation, no insider unlocks** — the pump.fun protocol structurally prevents them
- Curve completion → token auto-migrates to PumpSwap with LP locked permanently

The Snake OS team will buy on the launch curve the same as everyone else. The treasury will make a small purchase at launch to fund future rewards and operations.

## Tokenomics

**After launch, the weekly burn pool runs continuously:**

| Source | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Weekly burn pool | 2,000,000 tokens / week |
| Funded by | Treasury holdings + PvP/market fee conversions |

Users burn their BITS each week for a proportional share of that week's pool. The more BITS you've accumulated, the larger your share.

**Treasury token uses:**
- Weekly BITS burn pool payouts
- Achievement claim rewards (when token-denominated claims open)
- Operations (gas, infrastructure)

## Who Can Claim Tokens

When token-denominated claims open, every eligible endpoint will require:
1. A connected Solana wallet
2. A non-zero balance of the token in your wallet at claim time

> Holding any amount qualifies — no minimum size. This filters airdrop farmers while skipping ATA-creation rent for the platform (~0.002 SOL per claimer saved).

BITS-to-token exchange via the weekly burn pool does **not** require holding the token first — the burn pool is open to anyone with BITS.

## Holder Multiplier

Token holders get a bonus multiplier on their weekly BITS burn. At settlement, your **effective BITS = burned BITS × multiplier**. The pool is then split proportionally on effective BITS — holders earn a larger share without changing the total pool size.

| Tier | Tokens held | Multiplier |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Holder | 100,000+ (0.01% of supply) | 1.25× |
| Believer | 1,000,000+ (0.1% of supply) | 1.5× |
| Whale | 5,000,000+ (0.5% of supply) | 2× |
| No tokens | — | 1× |

**Balance is checked at week-end settlement** (Sunday midnight UTC), not at claim time. You can't buy tokens right before claiming then sell immediately — the snapshot already happened.

Hold in the wallet you connect with. Holdings in a different wallet don't count.

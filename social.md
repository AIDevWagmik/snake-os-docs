# Social Features

## 💬 Global Chat

Open with key `3` or the CHAT icon. One global room, rate-limited to prevent spam.

- Messages have a type: `chat` (normal), `global` (system), or `taunt` (in-game trash talk from PvP)
- Your equipped PFP and username appear next to every message
- BETA badge and equipped cosmetic badge display inline
- Sending 50 messages across all time unlocks the Chat Regular achievement (+1,500 BITS)
- Each message you send earns BITS (daily cap applies)

---

## 👥 Friends

Find and add other players. Shown in the FRIENDS app.

- **Search** by username or wallet address
- **Add** — sends a friend request; they get a notification
- **Accept** — confirms the friendship
- Friend list shows: equipped PFP, BETA badge, equipped cosmetic badge, online presence

**5 friends total** unlocks the Friendly achievement (+2,000 BITS).

---

## 🔔 Notifications

The notification badge on the FRIENDS and PVP home-screen icons shows unread counts. Types:

| Type | Trigger |
|------|---------|
| friend_request | Someone added you |
| pvp_invite | You were invited to a PvP match |
| rematch_invite | Opponent requested a rematch |

Notifications poll every 10 seconds. Dismiss individually or all at once.

# Who Earns $SNAKE

See [Reward Eligibility](../rewards/eligibility.md) for the complete policy.

The TL;DR for $SNAKE specifically:

## Three Paths to Earning $SNAKE

| Path | What you do | What you earn |
|---|---|---|
| **PvP** | Win PvP matches | SOL winnings + weekly $SNAKE prize-pool distributions (top players by ELO) |
| **Market** | Buy any market item | Unlocks all your achievement $SNAKE claims (capital qualifies you forever) |
| **Hold** | Hold $SNAKE in wallet | Weekly holder buyback distributions (post-launch) |

## What You DO NOT Earn $SNAKE From

* ❌ Solo Snake play (cosmetic badges only)
* ❌ Logging in daily (no streaks, no daily quests)
* ❌ Adding friends (cosmetic achievement only)
* ❌ Sending chat messages (cosmetic achievement only)
* ❌ Referring others (no referral program)
* ❌ Just signing up (no "claim your first 100 $SNAKE" airdrop)

This is intentional. Every reward mechanism known to attract farmers is intentionally absent.

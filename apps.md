# Apps Guide

A quick reference for every app outside the core game and social features.

---

## 💼 WALLET

Your connected Solana wallet viewed inside Snake OS. Shows your SOL balance, recent on-chain activity, and your equipped PFP. Send and receive via your underlying wallet app — Snake OS never holds your private key.

Switching wallets: disconnect via the WALLET menu. Your username, badges, and game history are tied to your wallet address.

---

## 💸 BUY

Jupiter-powered swap for any SPL token. Paste a CA or pick from POPULAR chips (wrapped BTC, ETH, and selected meme tokens). Buying or selling on BUY earns BITS.

---

## 🔮 ORACLE — AI Risk Scanner

Two modes:

**Token scan (free, unlimited)** — paste any Solana CA to get:
- Risk verdict: SAFE / CAUTION / RISKY / LIKELY RUG
- Risk score 0–100
- Market cap, liquidity, 24h volume
- Flags: top-10 holder %, mint authority, freeze authority, low liquidity

**Free-form chat (metered)** — ask anything about Solana, memecoins, perps, or Snake OS. Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash. When RADAR is open with fresh data, chat reads the live RADAR snapshot for grounded directional reads. Spam protection: 4 messages in 8s triggers a cooldown.

Running an ORACLE session earns BITS. Each unique CA scanned earns a one-time BITS bonus.

---

## 📡 RADAR — Perp Signals

Live perp-signal aggregator across Hyperliquid and Phoenix. Read-only — it shows signals, it doesn't place trades.

| Tab | Content |
|-----|---------|
| SMART $ | HyperTracker smart-money roster + net positioning |
| MARKETS | Cross-venue funding deltas, OI, predicted funding |
| LIQS | Live liquidation tape (Hyperliquid + Solana perps) |
| WHALES | Taker fills ≥ $50k across 12 tracked coins |
| POSITIONS | Open books for 35+ named whale wallets |

Data refreshes: funding every ~60s, OI and fills via persistent server-side WebSocket (Durable Objects). Visiting RADAR earns BITS; 7 consecutive days unlocks the RADAR Watcher achievement.

---

## 🔥 DEGEN — pump.fun Feed

Three tabs:
- **MIGRATIONS** — tokens that just graduated from the bonding curve to PumpSwap
- **TRENDING** — highest volume/velocity on pump.fun right now
- **BOOSTED** — promoted listings

Tap any token to see details, then swap directly via Jupiter. Buying from DEGEN earns BITS.

---

## 🎯 SNIPE — KOL Wallet Tracker

Real-time feed of buys and sells from tracked Solana KOL wallets. See what notable traders are entering or exiting. Tap any token to open ORACLE for a risk scan.

---

## 📊 MARKET WATCH

Live price charts for SOL, BTC, ETH, and selected memecoins. Refreshes every 15–30 seconds. Good for checking macro direction before entering a perp or making a spot buy.

---

## 📈 STOCKS — Tokenized Equities

130+ tokenized real-world equities via Backed Finance xStocks — TSLA, NVDA, SPY, and more tradable on Solana during extended hours. Prices tracked live. Opens the asset in Jupiter to trade.

---

## 🐦 CT FEED

Curated X (Twitter) posts from Solana influencers and traders. Scrollable feed updated periodically. Tap to open the post in a browser.

---

## 💬 COMMUNITIES

Per-token discussion threads powered by CoinCommunities. Paste or scan a CA to open its thread. Good for checking sentiment on a project you just scanned in ORACLE.

---

## 🤣 MEMES

Vertical-scroll curated meme feed. Swipe up like TikTok. React with emotes — 20 reactions unlocks the Memeoor achievement.
